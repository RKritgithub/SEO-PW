SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing web pages to rank higher in search engine results. It improves website visibility, drives organic traffic, and enhances user experience.

Importance of SEO: 1.Increases website traffic : Higher rankings lead to more visitors. 2.Builds credibility and trust: Users trust top-ranked sites. 3.Improves user experience : Good SEO ensures better navigation and loading speed. 4.Boosts conversions and sales : More visitors mean higher chances of conversions.
